Q: When should I arrive?

A: We ask that you arrive at the boat by 4:45 PM for a 5:00 PM departure .

Q: Is there parking available near the port?

A: Yes, there is parking available near the port for guests arriving by car. You can visit the Casco Bay Lines website for more information about parking.

Q: Will there be food available during the reception?

A: Yes, we will have hors d'oeuvres available during the reception. Please let us know in advance if you have any dietary restrictions.

Q: Will there be drinks available during the reception?

A: Yes, we will have a full bar available aboard the boat. .

Q: Are children invited?

A: In general, we would prefer that children were not aboard. However, please contact us if you'd like to have your children join you. Children are allowed aboard the Bay Mist.
